Civil Association Research Group on the Multiple Management of Forest Ecosystems - GIMEFOR.

Since 1995, GIMEFOR has united a multidisciplinary team of specialists committed to conducting research and outreach activities. The group focuses on exploring alternatives for the multiple-use management of diverse forest ecosystems while fostering the integration of local communities into sustainable forest management practices. The various projects undertaken by this research group align with different areas of study.

About Us

Our Mision

"Our mission is to promote research, conservation, and the sustainable use of forest ecosystems through a multiple-use management approach, integrating science, innovation, and traditional knowledge. We are committed to generating nature-based solutions, enhancing forest resilience to climate change, and strengthening the participation of local communities and key stakeholders to ensure environmental sustainability and social well-being."

Our Vision

"To be a global reference in the research and sustainable management of forest ecosystems, promoting innovative and science-based solutions to address climate change, restore biodiversity, and strengthen the relationship between communities and forests. We envision a future where forest ecosystems are managed equitably, resiliently, and regeneratively, ensuring their conservation for future generations."

Our Values

Sustainability Science and Innovation Environmental Resilience Community Participation Equity and Environmental Justice Biodiversity Conservation Ethics and Transparency Forest Ecosystem Management Fair and Inclusive Forest Management
